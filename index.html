1. Maruti Suzuki

The brand is best known for its compact hatchbacks and compact SUVs with the Swift being one of the best-selling cars in their lineup as well as the country. Some of the other popular models from Maruti Suzuki include the Vitara Brezza, Alto 800, Wagon R, Baleno, Ertiga, S-Presso, and Dzire. Maruti also has a few premium models in their lineup - the Ciaz, S-Cross, and XL6 stand out for their high-end features specifications. With upcoming models like the Jimny, Grand Vitara, Swift Hybrid, and WagonR Electric, we expect Maruti Suzuki to retain their top spot for at least a few more years. Maruti Suzuki is the perfect choice for buyers who are on a budget and are looking to get their money's worth.

2. Hyundai

In the second place, with a market share of 16.41 percent, Hyundai is a manufacturer that has been growing in popularity in the country. Although the brand entered India in 1996, they hit the ground running with the launch of the original Santro in 1998. People were instantly impressed with what the South Korean manufacturer had to offer. Today, almost a quarter of a century later, Hyundai has been making headlines with their sporty hatchbacks, elegant sedans, and powerful SUVs. Listed as some of the best cars in the country, Hyundai has some impressive models under their belt.

3. Toyota

Arguably one of the biggest and oldest companies in India goes by the name of Tata. Originally founded in 1868, the Tata name has been a part of Indian history for over 150 years. Tata has affiliates in almost every sector including communications, consumer products, power, steel, air-conditioning, hotels, airlines, and they even managed to fit Starbucks into their very impressive list. However, we are here to look at the Tata Motors side of things which started back in 1945 and currently holds 8.82 percent of the market share in the country. With so much history behind them, it comes as no surprise that Tata is one of the best-selling car manufacturers in India, and since revamping their lineup, buyers have been paying a lot of attention to the vehicles that roll out every year.

5. Mahindra

This is a name that you can never leave out when it comes to automobiles in India. Mahindra & Mahindra was founded in 1945 and is one of the top 20 companies in the country.
